    How to Use the Recharge Card Scanner

    1. Enter your mobile number in the first input field.
    2. Enter the USSD prefix for recharging in the second input field.
    3. Click "Scan Recharge Card" and allow camera access when prompted.
    4. Point your camera at the recharge card and click "Capture".
    5. Wait for the app to process the image and extract the recharge key.
    6. If successful, click "Dial USSD" to initiate the recharge process.

    Note: Make sure the recharge card is well-lit and clearly visible in the camera frame for best results.
